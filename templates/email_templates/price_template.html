What Happens Next?

We will now arrange a cleaner to come and meet you and discuss the cleaning requirements. Your cleaner will now call you and make an appointment to come out to you at a mutually convenient time. We have attached our Client Handbook which has a number of useful pointers to help you to get the most from your cleaner, in particular, we draw your attention to the recommended products list that the cleaner will need whilst on your premises. For your first couple of cleans, we recommend that you leave a list as each Client has different requirements. Prior to your cleaner commencing, your agency fee must be in place by direct debit, in advance for insurance purposes as soon as you have agreed a start date.
